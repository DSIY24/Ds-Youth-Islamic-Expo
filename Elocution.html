Elocution Competition

  • •Open to all Senior students (Islamic and non-Islamic) only.
  • •This is an individual competition and consists of only one round
  • •Participants can choose one of the following topics:
  • • “The Influence of Role Models on Family and Community"
  • • “Balancing Independence and Responsibility in Teenagers"
  • • “The role of Education in Shaping Family and Community Values"
  • • “The Impact of Technology on Family and Relationships"
  • •Speech will be presented to a panel of judges.
  • •The time limit for the speech is 3 minutes.
  • •The speech (except Quranic verses and Hadith) may not be plagiarised as the speech should reflect the speaker's understanding and research. Points will be deducted for plagiarism. ​
  • •Participants should memorise their speech. Ayahs and hadith can be written on flash cards
